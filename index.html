Welcome to my practice. I provide diagnostic evaluations and ongoing treatment to patients suffering from a broad range of psychiatric conditions. Please take a few minutes to browse around my website. Here you will be able to learn more about me, my qualifications, treatment philosphy and other specifics about my practice. If you are considering scheduling a first time appointment please be sure to check out the "New Patient" and "Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)" sections of the site. If you have a question that is not answered on this site please contact me, even if you don't think that your question is very good. Chances are that if you thought of it then others are thinking the same thing. I will endeavor to post the answers to this site in a timely manner. If you have a specific question about your care, present or future, then you should contact my office by phone.
